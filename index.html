PortfolioForge is
Easy way to maintain your stocks

This app is your ultimate companion for smart investing. Track stocks, manage portfolios, and stay informed with current market price.

Holdings

Input all your stock investments along with buy and sell entries. Our app provides detailed investment analytics, including profit and loss calculations, for informed decision-making.

Dashboard

View overall stock counts, total investments, and overall profit/loss at a glance. Easily add follow-up actions for your stocks to stay organized and proactive

Privacy

Your data stays securely on your device. We prioritize privacy and do not track any of your stock information.

Watchlist

You can create unlimited groups to categorize your watchlist and organize your stocks effortlessly.
